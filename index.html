Drive Smart

Drive Smart is a video game designed to unconsciously teach the player about the harms of texting and driving in a playful way. The goal is to try to dodge as many rapidly falling text messages as you can. If efforts fail and the player hits one the game is over.


Drive Smart Version 1



Future

As a next step we began the process of designing a second version of this game where instead of the mouse moving the car your arrow keys would. In this verison your speed limit keep your score and every time you dodged a text the limit would go up by one. For every factor of ten you exceed your exit number would increase thus inviting more text messages to clutter your road. Again the goal would be to avoid as many text messages as possible.
